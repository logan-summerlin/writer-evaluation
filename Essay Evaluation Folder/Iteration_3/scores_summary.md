## Final Analysis: Key Improvement Areas for V4

### From Human Review (Priority)
1. **USE ACTIVE VOICE**: Especially in introductions and conclusions — stop using passive voice throughout
2. **Eliminate AI slop sentences**: No long meta-sentences like "raising fundamental questions about..." or "offers a lasting warning that..."
3. **Shorter sentences**: Keep average sentence length under 21 words; split long winding sentences into two
4. **Use section headers**: Separate arguments with clear headers
5. **Interesting thesis required**: Thesis must be compelling, not boring
6. **Factual precision**: Be precise about claims (e.g., "one of the largest" not "the largest")
7. **Mention relevant economic concepts**: Like Dutch Disease when explaining trade economics

### Strengths to Preserve
- Good arguments and reasoning (Essay 8)
- Compelling paradoxes when used (Spanish decline)

### From AI Review (Secondary)
1. Formulaic paragraph structures
2. Sections feel disconnected
3. Textbook-style delivery
4. Limited sentence variety
5. Lack of distinctive voice
