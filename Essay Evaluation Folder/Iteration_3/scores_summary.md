## Common Reviewer Observations

### Improvements from Iteration 2
- Better thesis statements and multi-causal arguments
- Stronger concluding sections that synthesize arguments
- Essays with moral conviction or paradox were more engaging
- Better logical explanations overall

### Persistent Weaknesses
1. **Formulaic paragraph structures**: Topic sentence → explanation → significance repeated mechanically
2. **Sections feel disconnected**: Not building organically toward thesis
3. **Textbook-style delivery**: Survey coverage rather than sustained analysis
4. **Limited sentence variety**: Prose feels mechanical despite attempts at variation
5. **Mechanical transitions**: Functional but not organic
6. **Lack of distinctive voice**: Competent but not memorable

### What Distinguished Top Essays
- Essay 10 (Haitian): Clear moral conviction, authorial engagement
- Essay 4 (Opium): Engaged with moral dimensions
- Essay 9 (Neolithic): Counterintuitive "ratchet effect" argument
- Essay 8 (Spanish): Compelling paradox of success breeding failure
